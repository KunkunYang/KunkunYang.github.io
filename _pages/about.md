* I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Digital Humanities, [School of Information Resource Management, Renmin University of China](https://irm.ruc.edu.cn/). My main research interests are Natural Language Processing, Digital Humanities, Digital Culture, E-Governance, Information Retrieval, and Machine Learning.
* I serve as the reviewer/PC member of Decision Support Systems, Signal Image and Video Processing, Computers Materials & Continua, AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence, and Pacific Asia Conference on Information Systems.
* I hold a Ph.D. in Information Systems from the [Department of Information Systems, City University of Hong Kong](https://www.cb.cityu.edu.hk/is/). My Ph.D. supervisors are [Prof. Juan Feng](https://www.sem.tsinghua.edu.cn/info/1183/32092.htm) and [Dr. David Jingjun Xu](https://www.cb.cityu.edu.hk/staff/davidxu/). I also hold an MSc in Machine Learning (with distinction) from [University College London, UK](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/) and a BSc in Applied and Computational Mathematics from [Jacobs University Bremen, Germany](https://www.jacobs-university.de/).
